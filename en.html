FAQ

Everything You Need to Know

Quick and direct answers to our customers' most frequent questions.

Is employee and payroll data secure? +
Yes, absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption protocols (AES-256) to protect all stored and transmitted data. We perform periodic backups to ensure no data is lost under any circumstances.
How is employee GPS location tracked? +
Location is only tracked at the exact moment of check-in and check-out via phone to verify the employee is within the predefined work zone (Geofencing). We never track employees throughout the day.
Can I upgrade or cancel my plan at any time? +
Yes, our plans are fully flexible. You can upgrade to access more features, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time without long-term commitments or termination fees.
